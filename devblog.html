Milestone 2 Sprint

November 14th - 20th, 2022

After our first sprint, we received feedback that color changing was not intuitive to use. This week, we prioritized implementing a wrist menu to solve this confusion.

We also received feedback that users wanted to be able to move at different speeds at different moments. To address this, we implemented a turbo movement mode and generally improved our movement controls. A button mapping is now available on our home page.

We also added movable sticky notes with keyboard input so that students can write longer legible notes.

Finally, we implemented a saving system since it is an essential part of retaining the notes our users create.





Milestone 1 Sprint

November 7th - 13th, 2022

Previously, in many of the classes we've taken in our university careers, we've felt that we needed a tool to help us visualize and annotate in 3D from inspecting different shapes in Topology classes to formulating molecules in Orgo classes. Virtual Reality is on the frontier of visual computing, so VR 3D note-taking is in a unique position to fulfill our needs for these classes, and hopefully for the billions of students worldwide, too.

In this week of development we focused on establishing our space and navigation controls. We also began implementing drawing ability including optimizing smooth paths, enabling color changing, and implementing world tracking.
